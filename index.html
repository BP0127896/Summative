Welcome to Food For Thought

Love cooking but stuck in a rut of using the same old recipes? Food For Thought will get you cooking delectable new dishes in no time.

We cut out the boring parts. The shopping, finding the recipes, even measuring out and weighing the ingredients.

Each box, which is delivered to your door, contains a recipe card with enough pre-measured ingredients and a step by step written tutorial and video tutorial to make the experience fun and exciting. There’s no food waste and you can keep your favourite recipes to cook again in the future.

What’s in the box?

You can choose from one of our many exciting food box recipes kits from the menu and become a professional chef in the comfort of your own home.

The end result

Whether you are an experienced cook or just starting off, Food for Thought will increase your current skills with our exotic and adventurous recipes and also make anyone with a basic knowledge of cooking into a master chef.
