Contact us

Food for thought in an online delivery service that operates within the United Kingdom and delivers our amazing food boxes straight to your doorstep. We are happy to receive any feedback that you would like to give us and your feedback will allow us to improve our service so we can make the experience even better for you in the future. You can drop us a message in the form below to let us know what you thought about food for thought. Your feedback is very important to us. We will try our best to respond to your email as fast as we can. You can also follow our Instagram profile to keep up to date with all our latest news and activities.
